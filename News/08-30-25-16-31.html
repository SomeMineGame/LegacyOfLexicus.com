The Legacy Of Lexicus wiki and other services have been moved to their own domain. SomeMineGame registered both legacyoflexicus.com and legacyoflexicus.net! What moved?
The wiki moved from https://someminegame.com/Lexicus/Home to https://legacyoflexicus.com. The data page at data.someminegame.com is now at data.legacyoflexicus.com. The same is true for the map.

Basically, whatever the url was, if it was related to the Legacy Of Lexicus, just change someminegame.com or someminegame.net to legacyoflexicus.com or legacyoflexicus.net, respectively. SomeMineGame's site will still be used for resources such as YouTube, his friends, or other cases not related to the Legacy Of Lexicus server.
